How to Use the Map

How does it work?






We intend for this resource to be used by tenants, advocates, and policymakers alike. The map can be used in three different ways depending your intents and purposes:

location_on

Know where your city is?

If you know where your city or unincorporated area is located on the embedded map, then simply click on the location and then click on the link in the pop-up window to view that city or area’s TBOR coverage.

help

Don't know where your city is?

If you don’t know where your city or unincorporated area is located on the embedded map, then type your address into the search bar located at the bottom of the map, click on the location, and click on the link in the pop-up window to view that city or area’s TBOR coverage.

location_city

Just browsing!

If you just want to browse grades and policies in cities and unincorporated LA County, then scroll through the list and click on the different cities to view their scores.


For tenants, advocates, and policymakers interested in joining the movement to enact TBOR policies in their cities and unincorporated areas, then please check out the Keep LA Housed Coalition’s website and social media for more information.

This project is a collaboration between the Anti-Eviction Mapping Project, Keep LA Housed Coalition, and Right to Counsel Coalition’s Research Committee.
